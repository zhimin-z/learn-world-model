# Frontier Debates: Language, Vision, and the Boundary of the World

> "Silicon Valley has been hypnotized by LLMs — completely hypnotized."
>
> — Saining Xie

This is a technical judgment — about the fundamental dispute between two definitions of "intelligence," a debate that will shape AI research for the next decade.

This lecture gives no verdicts. Five debates, each with its sharpest arguments laid out. The question is left for you.

- **Debate 1**: Is language a tool, or an "opiate"?
- **Debate 2**: What does the Bitter Lesson really say? Has LLM followed or violated it?
- **Debate 3**: Is AGI a goal, or a pseudo-problem?
- **Debate 4**: Are world models and LLMs competitors or complements?
- **Debate 5**: The era of "downloading humanity" — where does the data come from?
