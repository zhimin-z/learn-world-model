## What you will learn

<ul class="index-list">
  <li><strong>Why world models matter</strong> — historical context from Craik (1943) to JEPA.</li>
  <li><strong>Observation encoding</strong> — VAE, CNN encoders, ELBO, and the bridge into Dreamer.</li>
  <li><strong>Latent dynamics</strong> — GRU, MDN-RNN, and RSSM (deterministic + stochastic).</li>
  <li><strong>Architecture families</strong> — six families: RNN/RSSM, Transformer, Diffusion, JEPA, RWM, WAM.</li>
  <li><strong>Planning mechanisms</strong> — CEM-MPC, latent Actor-Critic, and TD-MPC.</li>
  <li><strong>Evaluation metrics</strong> — FID, reward correlation, consistency loss, PSNR, and horizon drift.</li>
  <li><strong>Frontier debates</strong> — language vs physical grounding, Bitter Lesson, AGI as a research target.</li>
</ul>
